This guide explains how digital freight forwarding uses technology to coordinate and manage shipments more efficiently than traditional methods. It covers services offered by road freight forwarders, including consolidation of shipments and real-time GPS tracking, along with benefits like enhanced visibility and cost reduction for both shippers and carriers.
