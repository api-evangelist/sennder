The article explains how supply chain visibility, the ability to track goods in transit from origin to destination, is essential for shippers in road freight logistics. It outlines best practices, technologies like GPS tracking and smart tachographs, and how embracing digital solutions helps companies overcome barriers to real-time shipment monitoring.
